Red Karahi

First wash chicken and add in wok with 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste and salt to taste till water dries. Then add ½ kg chopped tomato and 1 tbsp red chili powder and cook till tomato water dries. Now add 1 tbsp karahai masala, 1 tsp black pepper crushed, 1 tsp roasted white cumin and 1 cup oil to cook a little.
